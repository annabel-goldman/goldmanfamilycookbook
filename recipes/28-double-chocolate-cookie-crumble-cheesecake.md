# Double Chocolate Cookie Crumble Cheesecake

**Recipe by Anita Marcus**

---

## Ingredients

### For the Crust
- 8½ oz Famous Chocolate Wafers by Nabisco, crushed
- ¼ cup sugar
- ⅓ cup butter, melted

### For the Filling
- 3 (8 oz) packages of cream cheese
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 4 oz sweet baking chocolate, melted
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tablespoons half-and-half
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

---

## Directions

### For the Crust

1. In a medium bowl, combine the crushed chocolate wafers and ¼ cup sugar.
2. Add the melted butter to the wafer mixture and mix well.
3. Press the mixture into bottom and two inches up on either side of a 9-inch pan.

### For the Filling

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. In a large mixing bowl, blend the cream cheese, sugar, and flour together until light and fluffy.
3. Gradually mix in the melted chocolate.
4. Add the eggs one at a time and beat well.
5. Stir in the half-and-half and vanilla extract.
6. Pour the filling into the prepared crust.
7. Bake in the preheated oven at 400°F for 10 minutes.
8. Reduce the oven temperature to 325°F (163°C) and continue baking for 50-55 minutes, or until a wooden toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
9. Allow the cheesecake to cool for 15 minutes before serving.
